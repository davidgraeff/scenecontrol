Services may contain one of the following types as properties:
* bool : results in a checkbox
* int: results in a spinbox
* double, qreal: results in a doublespinbox
* int, range: results in a slider+spinbox
* strings: results in a textinput/dateedit/timeedit/datetimeedit
* enums : results in a Combobox
